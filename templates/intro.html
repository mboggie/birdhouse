birdhouse is a simple service that watches your timeline and saves it to a database, so you never miss anything.

  1. Sign in with your Twitter account below to activate the birdhouse service.
  2. Your home timeline will be monitored and stored behind the scenes. (Search, review, and customization features TBD.)
  3. If you revoke access to birdhouse using Twitter's Application Settings, birdhouse will delete the record of your use automatically.
